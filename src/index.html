FRIDA CAFÉ

Behind every great day is a cup of coffee

Welcome to FRIDA, where every sip and bite is a celebration of flavor and creativity. Inspired by the vibrant spirit of art and culture, our cafe offers a warm and inviting atmosphere for you to relax, recharge, and indulge.

At FRIDA, we serve carefully crafted specialty coffee, expertly brewed to satisfy the most discerning coffee lovers. If tea is more your style, we have a selection of finely sourced blends to soothe and refresh. Our cocktail menu is designed to add a little extra sparkle to your day, while our diverse food offerings—from hearty breakfasts to fresh salads, satisfying sandwiches, and delightful lunches—ensure there’s something for every craving.

Whether you're here for a quiet moment alone or catching up with friends, we strive to create a space where delicious food and drinks meet great company and good vibes. Come in and experience the perfect blend of taste and ambiance at FRIDA.
